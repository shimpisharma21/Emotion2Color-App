How Emotion2Color works

A simple three-step flow that connects your emotions with AI-powered color mapping.

1. Describe your emotion

Type how you feel in your own words — anything from “drained and tired” to “peaceful and grateful”.

2. AI interprets your mood

The Flask backend calls OpenAI to understand your emotional context and generate a matching color palette & composition.

3. Watch it become art

The React frontend paints those colors into a dynamic canvas and stores your mood in MongoDB as part of your journal.
